Conflict Detection Taxonomy

8-type conflict detection with severity grading and resolution strategies

#
Numeric Conflict HIGH

Detects contradictory numerical claims (e.g., "20%→8%" vs "reduced by ~50%"). Ratio > 1.1 triggers flag.

Resolver: flag_for_verification
T
Temporal Conflict MEDIUM

Detects time-based contradictions (outdated claims, contradictory date ranges for same entity).

Resolver: prefer_recent
E
Entity Mismatch MEDIUM

Same entity referenced with contradictory attributes. Chinese negation detection (有/没有, 是/不是).

Resolver: cross_reference
S
Scope Conflict LOW

Global claims vs regional/narrow findings (e.g., "all markets" vs "Chinese market").

Resolver: narrow_scope_preferred
C
Causal Conflict MEDIUM

Opposing causal claims (e.g., "X 导致 Y" vs "X 与 Y 无关").

Resolver: seek_additional_evidence
G
Granularity Conflict LOW

Fine-grained (quarterly) vs coarse-grained (annual) claims for the same metric.

Resolver: prefer_fine_grained
D
Definitional Conflict MEDIUM

Same term defined differently across sources (e.g., different definitions of "AI safety").

Resolver: clarify_definition
R
Source Reliability LOW

Large credibility gap between sources (official/paper=5 vs blog/wiki=1-2). Gap >= 2 triggers flag.

Resolver: prefer_higher_credibility
